Background

I’m an aspiring web designer who loves everything about the web. I live in London but I am originally from the lovely Provence region in south of France.
Since my move to London I've been working as a QA tester, first in the Video Game industry where I had the chance to test some of the most well-known video game series like Final Fantasy or Pokémon, and then as a Digital QA Analyst for Kaspersky Lab, one of the top anti-virus software companies. I’m excited to bring my life experience to the process of building fantastic looking websites.
