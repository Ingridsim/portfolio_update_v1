Background

I’m an aspiring web designer who loves everything about the web. I live in London but I am originally from the lovely Provence region in south of France. Since my move to London I've been working as a QA tester, first in the Video Game industry where I had the chance to test some of the most well-known video game series like Final Fantasy or Pokémon, and then as a Digital QA Analyst for a famous anti-virus software company. I’m excited to bring my life experience to the process of building fantastic looking websites.

I am always looking to learn new skills whether using online resources or the "old-fashioned way" by reading books, I am also a passionate photographer who can bring some photoshop skills into my web design projects.

I’d like to help creatives create an impressive online portfolio.
